Help

        
Welcome to the Dot1xer Supreme Configurator! This tool generates advanced AAA configurations based on best practices and vendor templates.

        
How to Use

        
    
          
  • Sidebar Navigation: Click the ? button to collapse/expand the sidebar and navigate through steps.
    • 
          
  • Platform Selection: Choose your device platform. Depending on the vendor and OS, different configuration options will be available.
    • 
          
  • AI Configuration Assistant: Optionally, use AI integration to ask questions about your configuration (simulated response).
    • 
          
  • Generate/Download: After completing the steps, generate your configuration and download it as a text file.
